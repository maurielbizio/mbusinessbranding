# Facebook Group AI

Part of the Mbusiness Branding AI brand.

---

## Purpose

Use the Facebook group as the primary organic channel to attract home service business owners, deliver value, and drive them into the lead magnet funnel (14-day free social content offer) which feeds the Founders VIP Program.

---

## Lead Magnet Funnel — Overview

**Offer:** 14 Days of Done-For-You Social Media Content (free)

**Goal:** Capture leads → nurture → convert to Founders VIP Program (10 spots, 75% off first month)

### Flow
1. Facebook group post / ad drives to **opt-in form** (short: name, email, phone, business, city)
2. Contact created in GHL → tags `lm-optin` + `lm-intake-sent` applied → enters Lead Magnet Funnel pipeline
3. **Intake form** (detailed: trade, service area, challenges, etc.) → triggers content build notification to owner
4. Owner builds content in Google Drive → pastes link on GHL contact → adds tag `lm-content-ready`
5. **14-day delivery + nurture sequence** fires automatically:
   - Day 0: Week 1 content (7 posts) delivered via Drive link
   - Day 3: Value email (competitor visibility)
   - Day 5: SMS check-in
   - Day 7: Week 2 content + Founders VIP Program pitch
   - Day 9, 11, 14: Objection handling → urgency → final close
6. **Google review request** fires 3 days after Week 2 delivery (SMS + email)
7. Leads who book a Strategy Call → flow into existing Strategy Call pipeline

---

## Content Strategy for Facebook Group

### Types of Posts That Drive Opt-Ins
- "Grab your free 14 days of done-for-you content" posts
- Pain-point posts: "Why your competitor is getting your calls"
- Social proof: before/after examples of home service business content
- Education: "What to post if you're a plumber / HVAC / roofer"
- Behind-the-scenes: showing how we build the content

### Posting Rhythm (suggested)
- 3-5x per week in the group
- Alternate: value post → social proof → CTA to opt-in
- Always drive back to the lead magnet opt-in form
