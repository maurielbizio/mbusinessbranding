# YouTube Research Folder

This folder is used to research YouTube videos in the Mbusiness Branding niche — studying competitors, top performers, and content strategies relevant to AI-powered services for home service businesses.

## Purpose

- Analyze high-performing YouTube videos in the marketing, AI, and small business niche
