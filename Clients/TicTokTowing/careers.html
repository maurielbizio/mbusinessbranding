Tow Truck Driver / Roadside Service Operator

About the Opportunity

We're looking for a motivated, reliable, and safety-conscious individual to join our growing towing and roadside assistance operation. This isn't just a driving job — it's a career. We'll train the right person from the ground up and give you everything you need to succeed in a recession-proof industry where the demand never stops. If you show up, work hard, and take care of customers, your income will reflect it.

Why Join Us?

  • No experience necessary — we invest in the right people
  • Unlimited income potential — the more calls you run, the more you earn
  • Learn a skilled trade with long-term career growth
  • Steady, in-demand work — vehicles break down 365 days a year
  • Be your own boss on the road — independence with solid support behind you

What You'll Do

  • Respond to service calls including vehicle tows, accident recovery, lockouts, fuel delivery, and tire changes
  • Safely operate tow trucks and recovery equipment on public roads and highways
  • Provide friendly, professional service to customers in stressful situations
  • Communicate clearly with dispatch and follow routing instructions efficiently
  • Conduct pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections
  • Maintain equipment cleanliness and report any mechanical issues
  • Complete service documentation and payment processing accurately

What We're Looking For

  • Valid driver's license with a clean driving record (this is a must)
  • Dependable, punctual, and self-motivated — you show up when you say you will
  • Ability to work in all weather conditions and handle physical tasks (hooking, lifting, directing traffic)
  • Strong communication and customer service skills
  • Willingness to learn — no experience required, full training provided
  • CDL or towing experience is a plus, but not required

Ready to Get Rolling?

If you're dependable, have a good driving record, and are ready to build something real for yourself — we want to hear from you.
